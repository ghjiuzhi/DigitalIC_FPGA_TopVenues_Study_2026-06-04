# Claim Language Risk Lexicon

## 高风险词

- `prove`
- `guarantee`
- `certify`
- `secure`
- `robust`
- `universal`
- `complete`
- `optimal`
- `full physical model`

## 推荐替换

- `prove` -> `show` / `indicate` / `is consistent with`
- `guarantee` -> `support`
- `certify` -> `evaluate under` / `diagnose`
- `secure` -> `security-relevant`
- `robust` -> `stable under the evaluated setup`
- `universal` -> `under the evaluated platform/setup`
- `complete model` -> `diagnostic evidence`

## 当前 RO-TRNG 稿件边界

- TDC：diagnostic/instrumentation evidence，不是 calibrated physical proof。
- Reduced-XOR：mechanism diagnostic，不是 full source model。
- SP800-90B：guidance/context，不是 certification claim。
- 第二块板：cross-board check，不是 FPGA-family conclusion。
