# ROTRNG Abstract Phrase Bank

这些是自写模板，不是论文原句。

## 背景句

- `Ring-oscillator TRNGs are attractive for FPGA-based security systems because they can be implemented with digital resources.`
- `Oscillator-based entropy sources are widely used when compact hardware randomness is required.`

## 问题句

- `However, output statistics alone do not determine which physical blocks belong to the measured entropy-source boundary.`
- `This assumption becomes fragile when restart behavior, sampling logic, and routed implementation affect the observed bitstream.`

## 方法句

- `This paper examines the boundary through a measurement chain that combines placement, restart, sampler-side counterfactuals, and timing diagnostics.`
- `We characterize whether the sampler-side implementation behaves as passive readout or as part of the measured source.`

## 证据句

- `The measurements show [bounded observation] under [platform/setup].`
- `The counterfactual results indicate [directional effect], while [control] limits a simpler alternative explanation.`

## 边界句

- `These results support a diagnostic boundary claim rather than a certification claim.`
- `The conclusion is limited to the evaluated FPGA setup and measurement protocol.`
