# Venue Scope and Sources

- TCAS-I: IEEE Transactions on Circuits and Systems I: Regular Papers (OpenAlex source S116977442)
- TCAS-II: IEEE Transactions on Circuits and Systems II: Express Briefs (OpenAlex source S93916849)
- TVLSI: IEEE Transactions on Very Large Scale Integration (VLSI) Systems (OpenAlex source S37538908)
- TC: IEEE Transactions on Computers (OpenAlex source S157670870)
- TCAD: IEEE Transactions on Computer-Aided Design of Integrated Circuits and Systems (OpenAlex source S100835903)
- CHES/TCHES: IACR Transactions on Cryptographic Hardware and Embedded Systems (OpenAlex source S4210207404)

Selection rule: prefer 2024-2026, allow 2023 when needed to reach 10 directly downloadable open PDFs, and prioritize FPGA, ASIC, VLSI, SoC, accelerator, RISC-V, EDA, memory/CIM, and cryptographic hardware. PDFs are from explicit open versions such as IEEE OA, institutional repositories, arXiv, and IACR/TCHES.
